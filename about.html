Flora

Flora Group sprouted from a tile cart in Melattur in Malappuram with the idea to improve the tiles scenes in the area. Our team specializes in retailing and wholesaling business of tiles sanitary and granites. We have been in the industry in Years. It is the standing trust of our customers which made us reach so far. Flora ceramic,Flora gallery and Flora Tiles ventures originated from Flora Group. Our primary focus is to purchase best quality products and supply it to customers at an affordable rate. We deliver the product to end user at a lower price by reducing the overall expenditure.since we give importance to both quantity and quality. We supply large quantity of end products to our clients without compromising the quality. This is possible by reducing the over head cost production expenses. Even our sanitary ware consists of wide range of visually pleasing designs.the idea to improve the tiles scenes in the area. The cart was quite the success, with more fans lined up.
